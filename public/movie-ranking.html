The Best Movies According to ROGITH

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Fight Club

In this provocative drama co-starring Brad Pitt and Edward Norton and directed by David Fincher, a disaffected man and his charismatic friend organize brutal, bare-knuckle boxing matches.

Batman Collection

Batman is called Batman because Bruce Wayne chose the name to strike fear into the criminals of Gotham City. He was afraid of bats, and by adopting the persona of a bat, he used his greatest fears against his enemies

Ford v Ferrari

The Ford vs Ferrari rivalry is famously depicted in the film "Ford v Ferrari" (2019), which tells the story of American car designer Carroll Shelby and driver Ken Miles as they work to build a revolutionary race car for Ford to defeat Ferrari at the 24 Hours of Le Mans in 1966.
